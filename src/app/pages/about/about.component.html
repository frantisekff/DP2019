About

The website was created as pracital part of thesis. This thesis has a educational meaning. We implemented attacks to classical historical ciphers. Brute force attack to Caesar cipher, attack to Vigenere cipher with statistics of language, solving of monoalphabetic cipher using Hill Climbing alghorithm. The important part of this thesis is graphic representation of attack. The effectuivity and speed is not to important.
