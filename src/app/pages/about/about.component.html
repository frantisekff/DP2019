About

This website was created as a practical part of the thesis. This thesis has the educational meaning. We implemented attacks on classical historical ciphers. Brute force attack on Ceasar cipher, attack on Vigenere cipher with the statistics of language, solving of the monoalphabetic cipher by using a Hill climbing. The important part of this thesis are graphical representations of attacks. An effectivity and a speed are not important.
