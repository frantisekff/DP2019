About

The website was created as a practical part of the thesis. This thesis has an educational meaning. We implemented attacks on classical historical ciphers. Brute force attack to Caesar cipher, attack to Vigenere cipher with statistics of language, solving of monoalphabetic cipher using Hill Climbing algorithm. The important part of this thesis is a graphic representation of attack. Effectivity and speed are not important.
